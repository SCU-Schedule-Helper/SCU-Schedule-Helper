How We Use Your Data

SCU Schedule Helper requires Google OAuth sign-in to verify that you are an SCU student and to personalize your experience. We use your Google account solely for authentication and for exporting your course schedule to Google Calendar (if you consent). We do not store or share your calendar data, and we only access it to prevent duplicate events.

Additionally, we collect and store your course history and saved schedules if you choose to sync them with your profile. This enables features such as course recommendations and seeing what classes your friends have taken. All data is handled securely and is never shared with third parties.
